A bit more about me

Data science BA from UC Berkeley, currently a Master's student at Trinity College Dublin studying Business Analytics and AI.

Long-term, I want to build a career at the intersection of people and data, figuring out what drives how people work, decide, and behave, and then building something useful out of it.

Outside of school and work, I'm probably planning a trip, mid-trip, or recovering from one. Traveling is one of the things I love most in life, and I'm always down to experience a new culture, flavor, or time zone.

When I'm not traveling, you'll find me hanging out with dogs, at a hot pilates class, or attempting a TikTok DIY project. I'm also weirdly obsessed with productivity systems, like Notion pages, bullet journaling, or anything color coded. Basically, I'm running a tiny people-analytics project on myself at all times.
